Why was it impounded?

The PLATE corresponds to the registration of your vehicle, the FINE is the equivalent of the offense to be paid to the police, DEPOT is the cost of storage inside the warehouse (it is charged from the moment the vehicle was immobilized) and PARKING is the pending cost if the vehicle was in a parking lot (the price is charged only until before the moment the vehicle was immobilized)

The total cost does not include the parking fee while you review this menu
